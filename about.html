About

The Restaurant

Majken's Kitchen was founded in the autumn of 2020 with the main focus on serving good food and drink. Through the use of organic locally produced food, we also contribute to protecting the environment. Much of the food served is actually grown in the garden and in greenhouses at Majken's Kitchen. The food and drink we do not produce ourselves, we buy from producers and suppliers in the local area. During the summer, the table is set in the garden and guests can enjoy a good dinner in the sun. During winter, guests are welcome into the house where a warm tiled stove keeps the cold out.
